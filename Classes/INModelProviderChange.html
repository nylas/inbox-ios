Overview

An instance of INModelProviderChange encapsulates a single change to the items provided by an INModelProvider. You can use the information in an INModelProviderChange to update your UI in a granular way, ie. updating a particlar message row rather than refreshing the entire view.

To get a changes, implement INModelProvider’s -provider:dataAltered: delegate method.
